# User Walkthrough - ToolWise

Welcome to ToolWise! This guide will help you get the most out of our AI Architect.

## 1. Generating Your First Blueprint
- **Describe Your Idea**: Be as specific as possible (e.g., "A real-time dashboard for tracking greenhouse gas emissions using IoT sensors").
- **Select Constraints**: Choose your difficulty level and time availability.
- **Set Your Goal**: Are you here to learn every detail ("Deep Learning") or build it as fast as possible ("Rapid MVP")?

## 2. Exploring the Results
- **Tech Stack**: Review the recommended tools. Hover over them to see why they were chosen and their current market demand.
- **Compare Strategies**: Use the toggle to see an alternative approach (e.g., swapping a complex DB for a simple one).
- **GitHub Inspiration**: Click "Find Similar Projects" to see existing high-quality codebases that match your stack.

## 3. Using the Success Path
- **Step-by-Step Roadmap**: Follow the phases from "Knowledge Foundation" to "Deployment".
- **Interaction**: Click on a node to see detailed concepts and curated YouTube/Google tutorials.
- **Progress Tracking**: Click the "Circle" icon to mark a phase as complete. Your progress is auto-saved to your profile.

## 4. Deep Research & Architecture
- **Start Deep Research**: Generate a technical report on competitors and monetization.
- **Code Guide**: Open the "Step-by-Step Code Guide" to see actual boilerplate code and shell scripts to initialize your project folder structure.

## 5. Persistence
- **Library**: All your generated blueprints are saved in the "Dashboard".
- **Resume Tip**: Every blueprint comes with a tailored "Resume Power Liner" you can copy and paste directly into your portfolio or LinkedIn.

## 6. Architect Chat
- **Stuck?** Use the Architect Chat at the bottom right. It knows your specific project details and can explain the "how" and "why" behind any part of the architecture.
